Who are we?

We are a non-profit review webpage for almost all existing Monster energy flavours sold in Sweden. Here you'll find information about how the varioud flavours taste, how refreshing they are, in what situations they are perfect to drink and more. If you want to get started, check out our various reviews which are linked to at the top of our page!

Why choose us?

Unlike other reviewers that you may find online, our review team is carefully picked out who has established themselves as taste critics for items such as snacks and refreshments. All reviews that are performed are organized to make sure all participants agree with each other on their collective conclusion about said flavor. (No we are definietly not like 3 teens who drink way too much monster)

So, what are you waiting for? Go and find your perfect Monster energy flavor!
